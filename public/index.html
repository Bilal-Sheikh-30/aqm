AIR QUALITY MONITORING

Breathe Better. Live Healthier.

Project Overview

The goal of this project is to design and implement an IoT-based air quality monitoring system that leverages the capabilities of the ESP32 microcontroller and a DHT11 sensor to measure and report environmental conditions such as temperature and humidity. This project is aimed at monitoring air quality in real-time, providing valuable data for various applications, including indoor air quality control and environmental monitoring.

System Functionality

Real-Time Monitoring: The system collects data from the DHT11 sensor and transmits it to the cloud platform at regular intervals.

Data Display: The data will be visualized in an intuitive and user-friendly web dashboard, where users can track temperature and humidity levels over time.

Alerting: The system can be configured to send alerts if the air quality falls outside predefined thresholds for temperature or humidity.

Applications

Indoor Air Quality Control: The system can be used in homes, offices, or industrial settings to monitor and maintain optimal indoor air quality.

Environmental Monitoring: By monitoring temperature and humidity, this system can provide insights into environmental conditions, helping researchers and environmentalists assess climate change impacts.

Smart Home Integration: The system can be integrated into smart home systems, where it can trigger actions like turning on fans or air purifiers when air quality falls below acceptable levels.
